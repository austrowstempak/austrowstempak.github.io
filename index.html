How we met

I first saw Nicole at a professional networking event in 2016. Her orange dress and purple underlights caught my eye. We had a great discussion about data privacy issues, among other topics, until I abruptly exited the conversation, which led her to believe I had disliked talking to her. I also neglected to obtain any contact details and simply assumed she would attend the monthly follow-up events (spoiler: she did). At the third one, I finally asked her to come out for a drink with some of my friends, which was an utter disaster. For some reason, she later agreed to a real date and we spent over eight hours touring multiple museums, with dinner in Little Italy and a final drink at a grungy Cleveland Heights bar. It would be another three months before we discussed whether we were, in fact, dating.
